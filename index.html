It combines its previous experience in the private sector with today's technology and closely follows local and international developments in its sector. The work is carried out in 25,000 m² of steel manufacturing It relies on providing services based on international quality in the field and aims to be a leading company in the field. Çelikerler provides services in a number of fields Steel Structure (Prefabricated House - Steel Construction House - Heavy Industry Buildings -Container - Construction Site Structures), Steel Mosque Dome MinaretIt has expanded the boundaries of quality and service in the field and signed many Successful projects in the country and abroad. OUR VISION

To be a well-known company in the international market, a leader in the national market and one of the top 100 companies in the Turkish industrial sector specialized in steel and prefabricated structures.

OUR MISSION

To design and build high quality, reasonably priced, modern, trusted and environmentally friendly steel and prefabricated structures.
